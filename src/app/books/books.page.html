What I Talked About When I Talk About by Haruki Murakami Genre: Biography Haruki Murakami shares his thoughts on the connection between his life as a writer and his love for running. The book is a mix of personal stories and reflections, exploring how the discipline of running influences his creative process and offers insights into life's journey.
Sophie's World by Jostein Gaarder Genres: Novel, Philosophical fiction "Sophie's World" is a novel by Jostein Gaarder that tells the story of a teenage girl named Sophie who discovers the world of philosophy through mysterious letters. The book takes readers on a journey through the history of Western philosophy in an engaging and accessible way, making complex ideas easy to understand.
